July 13, 2023 Signs of the Times The iconic Hollywood sign was unveiled 100 years ago today, though it had an extra syllable back then. The towering letters spelled “Hollywoodland” to advertise a nearby housing development. But 20 years later, the sign had fallen into disrepair, so the city of Los Angeles purchased and renovated it, nixing “land.” It’s a rags-to-riches story worthy of its locale—from shabby ad to entertainment industry icon.
